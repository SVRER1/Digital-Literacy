# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
Canva (canva.com) – free browser-based graphic design platform

## What the Infographic Shows

The infographic is titled "Be Digitally Literate: A Guide for First-Year Students" and covers three core topics:

1. What is Digital Literacy?
   Defined as the ability to find, use, evaluate, and communicate information using digital technology responsibly and effectively.

2. Useful Digital Tools for Students
   - Google Workspace (Docs, Drive, Meet) for collaboration
   - Canva for design and presentations
   - GitHub for version control and portfolios
   - HackerRank for coding practice

3. Safe Internet Practices
   - Use strong, unique passwords
   - Enable two-factor authentication
   - Be careful about what you share online
   - Verify links before clicking

## Design Approach
The design uses a blue and white colour scheme with icons for each section, making it easy to scan quickly. Short bullet points were used instead of long paragraphs so peers can absorb information at a glance.
