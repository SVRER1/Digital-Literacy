## Overview

This project was developed as part of the Digital Literacy course. It is based on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, where the objective is to create awareness about digital tools, online safety, and professional digital presence.

The project consists of five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy, including infographic creation, portfolio building, platform exploration, communication skills, and cybercrime awareness.

## Tasks Summary

### **Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic**

Created an infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy, useful tools, and safe internet practices in a visually clear format.


### **Task 2: Digital Portfolio**

Developed profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a professional online presence and showcase learning goals.


### **Task 3: Platforms Exploration**

Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to assess digital literacy awareness.

### **Task 4: Email Etiquette**

Prepared a case study on phishing and developed a prevention checklist to promote safe online practices among students.
