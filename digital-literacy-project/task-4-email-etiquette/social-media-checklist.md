# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO's

| # | Do This |
|---|---------|
| 1 | Keep your profiles professional — future employers and internship coordinators will look you up online. |
| 2 | Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts, photos, and personal information. |
| 3 | Think before you post — ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if my professor or parent saw this?" |
| 4 | Use social media to share your achievements, projects, certifications, and learning milestones. |
| 5 | Verify news and information before sharing — spreading misinformation can have serious consequences. |
| 6 | Report cyberbullying or harassment using the platform's reporting tools and to cybercrime.gov.in or helpline 1930. |
| 7 | Keep your contact details (phone number, home address) off public profiles. |

## DON'Ts

| # | Avoid This |
|---|------------|
| 1 | Do NOT share your OTP, password, Aadhaar number, or bank details on any platform, even in private messages. |
| 2 | Do NOT accept friend/follow requests from strangers without verifying their identity. |
| 3 | Do NOT post about travelling or being away from home in real time — this is a safety risk. |
| 4 | Do NOT engage in online arguments, trolling, or hate speech — it reflects on you permanently. |
| 5 | Do NOT click on suspicious links sent via DMs, even from people you follow. |
| 6 | Do NOT tag others in embarrassing or private photos without their explicit consent. |
| 7 | Do NOT use the same password across multiple social media platforms. |

---

**Reporting Channel:** National Cyber Crime Portal – cybercrime.gov.in | Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
